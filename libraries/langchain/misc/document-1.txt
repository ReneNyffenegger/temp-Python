Surfers along the coast often gather to catch waves, and among them is Atticus
Stormrider.  Leading campaigns to protect local wildlife is a passion that
drives Lyric Firestone.  When it comes to tennis, the boss of Seraphina
Halloway is a true enthusiast, often hosting matches.  Despite being somewhat
overlooked, Nova Ironwood has created groundbreaking gadgets as a brilliant
inventor.
